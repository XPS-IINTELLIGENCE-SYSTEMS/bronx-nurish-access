FAQ

Common questions

Is there a payment to check?

No payment is required to check available food help options.

Does this guarantee food delivery?

No. Screening does not guarantee eligibility, enrollment, delivery, meal quantity, duration, Medicaid approval, SNAP approval, or any specific service.

Who should I call?

For Bronx Social Care Network screening, call 1-833-SOMOSNY.

What should I avoid entering?

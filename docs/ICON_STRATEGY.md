# Bronx Nourish Access PWA Icon Strategy

Purpose: create a phone home-screen icon that stands out immediately, feels trusted, and makes the app easy to share like a digital community help card.

## Icon Rules

- Must be readable at small size.
- Must use high contrast.
- Must not look like a generic health app.
- Must feel urgent but safe.
- Must communicate food help, care, Bronx/community, and action.
- Must avoid official government-program impression unless verified.

## 10 Strong Icon Concepts

### 1. Green Shield + Gold Leaf
Psychology: safety, trust, protection, food access.
Best for: official-looking but not government-looking.
Risk: can feel healthcare-heavy if too plain.

### 2. Orange Help Button + White Grocery Bag
Psychology: urgent action, instant CTA, easy recall.
Best for: fastest visual recognition.
Risk: orange must not overpower trust.

### 3. Heart-Shaped Apple + Bronx Green Circle
Psychology: care, food, family, emotional warmth.
Best for: community sharing and family audience.
Risk: may feel too soft if not bold enough.

### 4. Hand Holding Leaf + Gold Sunburst
Psychology: help, hope, nourishment, growth.
Best for: inspirational share behavior.
Risk: more detailed, needs simplification at small size.

### 5. Bold BNA Letters + Leaf Cutout
Psychology: brand memory and home-screen readability.
Best for: repeat use and field workers.
Risk: less obvious that it is food help.

### 6. Grocery Bag + Medical Cross Alternative
Psychology: food support plus health-plan routing.
Best for: Medicaid/health-plan adjacent funnel.
Risk: avoid official medical/government feel.

### 7. Bronx Map Pin + Fork/Leaf
Psychology: local, immediate, place-based support.
Best for: Bronx-specific campaign.
Risk: map pin can feel like delivery app.

### 8. Gold Circle + Green Checkmark + Apple
Psychology: qualification, progress, relief.
Best for: “check options” CTA alignment.
Risk: checkmark must not imply approval guarantee.

### 9. Family Silhouette + Food Basket
Psychology: household help, family protection, dignity.
Best for: emotional community campaign.
Risk: more complex at app-icon size.

### 10. Emergency Food Card Icon
Psychology: saved card, help pass, shareable resource.
Best for: digital business card strategy.
Risk: must avoid benefit-card/government-card confusion.

## Recommended Top 3

1. Green Shield + Gold Leaf
2. Orange Help Button + White Grocery Bag
3. Emergency Food Card Icon

## Recommended Active Icon

Use Green Shield + Gold Leaf first because it balances trust, food, and safety without making unverified government claims.

## Share CTA Language

Primary share button:

Share This

Supporting copy:

Help another Bronx household check available food help options.

SMS / WhatsApp copy:

Bronx families may be able to check available food help options here: [LINK]

Do not use:

- guaranteed meals
- guaranteed approval
- federal government paid
- 3 meals per day
